WHAT SEPARATES
FAST FASHION from SLOW FASHION
It delivers low-cost apparel quickly. It delivers high-quality apparel at a fair cost.
It appeals to consumerism, hiding the impact of your frenetic consumer behaviour. It promotes consciousness at each purchase, making you realize its impact.
It uses simple seductive visuals to stimulate illusional needs. Through narrative elements it shows the bad side of fashion.
It sells seasonal trendy and flashy products. It sells classic products that last more than one season.
Manufacturing standards beyond the boundaries of sustainable and ethical norms. Ethical manufacturing standards with a reduced environmental impact.
It values low costs and quantity over quality. It values ethics and quality over quantity.
